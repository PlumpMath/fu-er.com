I am a coder on the outside, artist on the inside, trying to turn inside out. This website is step one of my metamorphosis. One day, I will emerge as a fantastical hybrid with ten thousand hours of passion-fueled experience in my pocket. Until then, I will continue to refine my skill one step at a time.

Still uncertain of what type of art I want to do, my most immediate goal is to bridge my engineering background and my art future. This should manifest itself as some kind of technical artist role or catch-all indie game developer.

Some other facts about me in a nice, condensed format:

  • started making clay miniatures in 2008
  • actively dabbles in Illustrator/Photoshop/3dsMax
  • has game industry experience, particularly in mobile games
  • loves working with UI artists
  • has roots in Singapore, but has spent the last 2 years in Silicon Valley

Feel free to contact me directly if you have any other questions, or to leave a few words of encouragement.
